About Me

I've been a software engineer with Apple for 4 years, working mostly on the iCloud Mail web client. Before that, I worked for a stock photography startup called Twenty20. Unfortunately, that company was acquired and later shuttered.

Much earlier, I worked in the music industry as a sound engineer. I recorded and mixed artists across genres, and did some composing/scoring for commercials (and one film).

EventuallyI went back to college, studying International Relations at the University of Southern California, and International Economic Policy from George Washington University. These subjects aren't related to what I do now, but I still consider them a valuable education and experience.

After a short time working in managment consulting (which I strongly discourage), I taught myself to code. Starting with basic web development, I learned quickly and accumulated quite a few knowledge gaps. But my priority was getting just competent enough to land a job, regardless of how much learning debt I accrued. It paid off, as Twenty20 hired me after roughly 6 months. The engineering manager took a chance on easily the greenest developer, which let me grow and learn from skilled and experienced engineers.

I've always been a passionate learner, if not a great student. The projects detailed on this site illustrate some of the interests I've pursued.
