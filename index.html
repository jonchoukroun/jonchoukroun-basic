About Me

I've been a software engineer with Apple for 4 years, working on the iCloud Mail web client. Before that, I worked for a stock photography startup called Twenty20. Unfortunately, that company was acquired and later shuttered.

Much earlier, I worked in the music industry. I recorded and mixed artists across genres, and did some composing/scoring for commercials.

Eventually I went back to college, getting my bachelor's from University of Southern California and my master's from George Washington University. Although I studied Internal Relations and Economics, my work today has no relation on my degrees.

After a short time working in managment consulting (which I highly discourage), I taught myself to code. I started with basic web development, and I landed the startup gig within about 6 months. They really took a chance on the greenest developer, but it allowed me to build my skills and learn.

I've always been a passionate learner, if not a great student. The projects highlighted on this site illustrate what I've focused on teaching myself.
